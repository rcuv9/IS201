About Me

My name is Ryan Cuvelier. I was born in Naperville, Illinois on February 1, 2002.

I have lived most of my life in Davis, California. When I was 14, I moved to Curitiba, Brazil, where I lived for three years and attended high school. At 17, I moved to Katy, Texas where I completed my senior year of high school. Soon after, in 2020, I attended a year of college at Brigham Young University before serving a mission in Joao Pessoa, Brazil for two years.

I am currently a sophomore at Brigham Young University and will study finance. Aside from finance, I love to play sports and spend time with family and friends. I currently play on the BYU Men's Soccer Team where I am a midfielder and forward. I have played soccer my whole life and love to be around the game.

If you would like to learn more about me and get to know me better, feel free to contact me or connect with me on LinkedIn.
